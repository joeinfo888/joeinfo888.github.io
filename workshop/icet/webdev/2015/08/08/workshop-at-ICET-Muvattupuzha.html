Workshop at ICET Muvattupuzha

08 Aug 2015 on workshop, icet, and webdev

Today I took a workshop on web development to the B tech CSE students of ICET Muvattupuzha. The audience was very receptive and enthusiastic about learning more about webdevelopment. The plan was to cover HTML and CSS and give introduction to Javascript and PHP. Experienced people may say it is very ambitious to cover all these topics in a day. I believe I did a good job in covering HTML and an introduction to CSS. Still waiting for the feedback. I will be uploading the presentations I prepared for the workshop soon.

My University exams are starting on 11th of this month and will be over by 19th of this month. I am hopeful that I will be able to upload the presentations before 23rd of this month.
